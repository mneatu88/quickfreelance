# Project Overview

**QuickFreelance**  
**The freelance marketplace + academy where freelancers teach freelancers.**

**Location:** Leeds, West Yorkshire, United Kingdom  
**Founded:** 2026  
**Status:** Beta (Live)

---

## What is QuickFreelance?

A dual-platform SaaS that combines:
- A modern **freelance marketplace** (gigs with 10% commission)
- An **Academy** where freelancers create and sell courses to other freelancers (0% commission on course sales)

Our mission: Help freelancers earn more money and learn faster — all in one place.

---

## Key Differentiators

- Built-in education-to-earning loop (Academy → Marketplace)
- **0% commission on Academy courses** — mentors keep 100% of course revenue
- Strong focus on "How to Sell Online" (Amazon, TikTok, eBay)
- Clean, premium UI/UX

---

## Revenue Model (Transparent)

Even though we charge **0% commission** on Academy course sales, we generate revenue through:

- **Subscriptions** paid by **both students (cursanți)** and **mentors/freelancers**
  - Starter plan: £9.99/month
  - Pro plan: £24.99/month
- **10% commission** on Marketplace gig orders

This model incentivizes high-quality course creation while ensuring sustainable platform revenue.
